#tab completion in ipython and Jupyter Notebooks 

pressing the tab key will search the namespace for any variable (objects, functions, etc) matching the characters you have typed so far: 

In [1]: an_apple = 27

In [2]: an_example = 42

In [3]: an<TAB> 
an_apple and 		an_example

In [4]: b = [1,2,3]

In [5]: b.<TAB>
b.append	b.count
b.clear		b.extend
b.copy 		b. index

same goes for modules! 

In [1] import datetime

In [2] dataframe.<TAB> 

datetime.MAXYEAR       datetime.datetime      datetime.timedelta
datetime.MINYEAR       datetime.datetime_CAPI datetime.timezone
datetime.date          datetime.time          datetime.tzinfo
